Poem 1

Get Along With

June 2020

Sometimes I just want to put a pretty dress, open up my hair
...and get transferred into another world very fair... where there i can..
Get along with the wind either calm or strong humming like the bees,
making me fly as i wanted to in my childhood fantasies...
Get along with the ocean that flows either harsh or slow
making me drenched in a way like the rain makes me imagine of... Get along with the clouds either fortunate or catastrophic,
that remind me of the girlhood dream heavenly and seraphic...
Get along with the trees either parched or foliate,
that dress me up like that of beautiful leafy princess's duplicate...
Get along with the flowers either blossoming or wilting
that gift me a crown as beautiful as of some distinguished's wreath but not representing my death...
Get along with the soil either black or brown
that scents me with its petrichorish smell as i loved to when the first rain fell...
Get along with the birds either flightless or fledged...
that i am presented with many new friends...
Get along with the animals either savage or tamed...
That provide me with their food of the type natural and unbaked...
Get along with the sky either cloudy or fair...
That enchants me with magical wings and i fly in its air...
Get along with the sun either moving or static...
That gives me the warmth and energy to overcome all the panic...
Get along with the stars either eternal or shooting...
that grants me a wish of lifelong happily living...
Get along with the moon either full or crescentic...
That makes my shoulders light and feathery by its gravity lethargic...

Poem 2

Can you listen

October 2019

Can you listen to the eyes? Those uttering their desire to touch the skies,
And the ones full of dreams so high.
The twinkling eyes of a little one that laugh aloud,
And the lifeless closed eyes among their last crowd.
For these eyes can say endless,
Unlike the mouth that may lie and mess.
The eyes of a beggar pleading for a pie,
The eyes of a complainter , can you listen to that why?
The devotee's eyes with hope and trust,
And a tired traveller with eyes full of thirst.
The eyes of your beloved with care and wish, and those special eyes with a bag full of kiss.
The eyes of a vendor who is eager to sell,
Can't you see in those eyes the desire for their family's well?
The eyes of a painter searching the perfect to copy,
Can't you tell that which of the given eyes are happy?
The eyes of an artist who can create those talking eyes,
And those painfull departing eyes with not so good 'goodbyes'
The eyes of a dreamer with colours of their own,
Don't you feel bad when you meet the eyes that moan?
The sparkling eyes after the great victory,
Do you notice the common eyes that create history?

Poem 3

Revealing a divine soul

August 2019

Oh mom! let me reveal a divine soul in words of my own,
As she is unaware how beautifully she rules this universal throne.
For the galaxies withhold the depth and sparkle of her eyes,
She is so twinklish that the stars radiate her vibes.
No doubt, she is the proud of this sky and the love of a selenophile,
cause they talk to the moon about her and the whole universe begins to smile.
She is like the lead character of that old fairytale,
Who heals every cut and bruise by her magical fingernail.
The dreams of her heart have a little favour for every being.
Our wishes come true when she flips her hand like amy magical wing.
She is more valuable a river of emotions than any gold and diamond,
The one whose face can heal your stress even if you are horizons beyond.
Every problem reminds of her be it big or the hair tangle of a girl little,
Had every heart imitated of hers' could make this world a home so sweet and tranquil.
These daily life 'hurricanes' and 'tornadoes' makes every tongue slip her name,
And still she so selfless is wondering being clueless that it's all about you my 'MOM'.
